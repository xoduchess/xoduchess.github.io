Shadouches "Duchess" Reed

A second year Master’s student at The Georgia Institute of Technology (Georgia Tech). She is studying Computer Science with a specialization in Visual Analytics, Human Computer Interaction. Ms. Reed was awarded the GEM Master’s Fellowship her senior year at Clark Atlanta University (CAU). While at CAU she became the schools’ first Microsoft Student Partner Tech. As a Microsoft Student Partner Tech she influenced students to get involved with different forms of technology. Ms. Reed currently is the IT chair for the Black Graduate Student Association (BGSA-GT). A Leadership Coach in the Leading Edge program at Georgia Tech, and a Mentor in the Peer to Peer program at Georgia Tech. She is also a member of The National Society of Black Engineers (NSBE) and Beta Kappa Chi (BKX) a national scientific honor society.
